About Workshop Manager

Workshop Manager is designed to transform how garages operate. By replacing fragmented, manual processes with a single, cloud-based digital hub, we help you reclaim hours of administrative time every week.

The Risk of Manual Diaries

  • Fragile: Paper records can be lost or damaged.
  • Siloed: Not accessible from the workshop floor.
  • Inefficient: Manual entry is slow and prone to errors.

The Power of Our Digital Hub

  • Secure: Encrypted data, automatic backups.
  • Synchronized: Reception, Parts, and Workshop linked in real-time.
  • Integrated: Job cards and parts requests managed in one dashboard.

Built For Every Workshop
